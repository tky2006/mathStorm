📖 How to Play

  • 🎯
    An equation with a missing value ? appears in the centre of the screen.
  • 🫧
    Five numbered bubbles fall from the top. Click the correct bubble that answers the equation!
  • ⏱️
    You have 15 seconds per question. Time out = lose a life and next round!
  • ❤️
    Start with 3 lives. Wrong click or timeout = lose a life. Zero lives = Game Over!

  • ⭐
    Earn 1–3 stars per round based on how fast you answer. Complete all 10 rounds to see your final rating!
  • 🧮
